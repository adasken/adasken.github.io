About Me

🤖

I'm a passionate researcher and data scientist currently pursuing my PhD in Computer Science at Griffith University. My work focuses on leveraging big data and AI to understand customer behavior in the digital age, particularly exploring how virtual influencers compare to human ones in marketing effectiveness.


With a strong foundation in machine learning frameworks like PyTorch and TensorFlow, I've developed hybrid models for traffic prediction and contributed to cutting-edge research in graph neural networks. I believe in the power of AI to transform industries and create meaningful customer experiences.


When I'm not diving deep into datasets or training neural networks, I enjoy exploring the Gold Coast and staying up-to-date with the latest developments in artificial intelligence and data science.
